Notes to Grader:

Feature I Added to make it "Awesome": Using these sources: webglfundamentals.org and learnwebgl.net, I implemented Alpha Transparency (using gl.BLEND) and a slider to control the opacity of my shapes. I also used gl.blendFunc(gl.SRC_ALPHA, gl.ONE_MINUS_SRC_ALPHA) which specifies the blending percentages. In other words, I enabled alpha blending in WebGL and modified the fragment shader to accept an alpha value. The slider allows users to adjust the transparency of the shapes they draw on the canvas.
